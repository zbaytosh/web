riting: I often write fiction about normal people in abnormal situations. Strange fiction (from superhero movies to horror novels, magical realism to fantasy and sci-fi) has a unique ability to explore the less accessible parts of everyday life, which is I think often what fiction writers are going for. A lot of what I try to do is bridge the divide between realism and the fantasy books I grew up with as a kid.
