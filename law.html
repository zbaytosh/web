aw: How can people solve social problems without resorting to violence? Legal systems---however imperfect---are humanity's best attempt to answer this fundamental question. Over the last couple of years I have become increasingly interested in the Supreme Court and its role in the U.S. government. I try to keep up with the oral arguments and opinions as they happen, and find it useful to write things down occaisonally. This page will act as a repository for general thoughts, case summaries, and interesting details I come across along the way.
