bout me: I graduated from Brown University in December of 2021, with a degree in philosophy and literary arts. My philosophy capstone was a critique of Frans de Waal's theory of animal morality. I also completed an honors thesis in literary arts, entitled The Fairy Queen. In June, I began a road trip around the continental United States, starting in New York. The trip ended in Rhode Island, at the Green Line Apothecary, on October 27th. Now, I am working, writing, reading and (eventually) applying to law school.

I was born in Denver, Colorado in 1999. Since then, I have lived in New York, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Virginia and Rhode Island. As a child, I wanted to be - at various times - an astronaut, a wizard and a writer. Unsurprisingly, I read a lot of fiction. Currently, I am reading The Upswing by Robert Putnam, The Federalist and Lemony Snicket's Poison for Breakfast.
