bout me: I graduated from Brown University in December of 2021 (degree awarded in May of 2022), with an A.B. in philosophy and literary arts. My philosophy capstone critiqued Frans de Waal's theory of animal morality, and responded to alternatives proposed by Christine Korsgaard and Philip Kitcher. I completed an honors thesis in literary arts, entitled The Fairy Queen. In early 2022, I began a road trip around the continental United States, starting in North Carolina and ending in Rhode Island, at the Green Line Apothecary.

I am currently applying to law school, and hope to start in the fall of 2024.

I was born in Denver, Colorado in 1999. Since then, I have lived in New York, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Virginia and Rhode Island.
